# Medications & Supplements

Complete medication and supplement inventory, including prescription drugs, over-the-counter supplements, and experimental compounds. Essential for identifying interactions and optimizing protocols.

## Format

For each medication/supplement, document:
- **Name**: Generic and brand name
- **Type**: Prescription / OTC / Supplement / Experimental
- **Dosage**: Amount and unit
- **Frequency**: Timing and daily total
- **Start Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
- **Indication**: Why you're taking it
- **Status**: Active / Paused / Discontinued
- **Notes**: Side effects, interactions, or protocol-specific notes
